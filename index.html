My Connections Puzzles

Hello! This is a collection of some Connections puzzles that I have created. Connections is a game created by the New York Times, and I used the concept and the public website Swellgarfo to create some of my own. I assume that you already know how to play Connections.

My puzzles have been tested and are playable at the Swellgarfo links below. I have added tags to the puzzles that I think are

  • hard,
  • extreme (very hard),
  • GPTS (good places to start), and/or
  • CE (mention current events as of the puzzle date).
As the tags show, I think I have become a better writer over time. (And my testers certainly agree!) As a puzzle writer, I try to avoid pop culture references, as it's not a good time to be stumped because you haven't watched a particular movie or read a specific book series, so I try to avoid them in my clues. Other than that, I hope you have fun! Happy solving!
